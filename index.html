About us

DreamZone School of Creative Studies is India's premier training institute for technology-enabled creative education in India. Through dedicated centres of excellence in creative skills training, we offer employment and entrepreneurship-oriented courses

Best services

We offer over 50 unique courses that are known for their comprehensive coverage and quality. Our experiential training methodologies are unique and the certifications are globally recognized. And our training services meet the quality standards of ISO (9001:2015).

fastest growing network

The first DreamZone came into presence in 2005. It was a single centre in Chennai, India. One of its first courses was on animation using Autodesk's 3DS Max. From this modest beginning, DreamZone became a 100 centre network by 2017, offering creative courses using over 25 world class software tools.

Unique franchising model

Instrumental to our phenomenal growth, is our unique franchising model. DreamZone forayed into franchising in 2009 with its no-royalty-fee franchising. A win win for franchisor and the franchise, our model has over 95% success rate - testimonial to our franchising success are our franchisees in many cities and towns.
